ABOUT US

At Bansal’s situated in Amritsar which is also known as Golden City of INDIA, we serve our customer’s specially hand picked ingredients to delight their taste buds. Our products are prepared in a home like environment where the old tradition of Roasting is still Alive.

Our Products have central ingredients mainly of Soya beans, Bengal Grams, Corn, Wheat, Pearl Millet and Peanut.We bring our raw ingredients from the best well known farms of India so that you need no to compromise with the taste of the product.
